## 1. Overview

The `plgg-ir-thesis-proof` package carried its own divergent copies of the
rebuttal / reachability / grounded-extension logic, drifting from the full
`compileThesis` evaluator the mission built. This unifies them: the proof now
rides the evaluator, so the diagnostic *is* the counterexample.

  them — per the repo's don't-clone-garbage direction.
- Rewrote the flagship examples (`debate.ts`, `rebuttal.ts`) as thesis-surface
  sources consumed by the full `compileThesis`: a complete rebuttal compiles →
  accept; a doctored source no longer compiles, refused with the evaluator's own
  ranged codes (`遮断`/`被覆`).

  instead of a re-implemented `AttackGraph`.
